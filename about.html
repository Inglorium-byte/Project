  • Mousse

    Description

    Mousse is your whimsical dessert buddy who knows how to keep things light and tasty! Each spoonful feels like a serenade from a sugar-laden symphony, charming your senses and making you grin like you’re in on some delicious secret. It’s not heavy or demanding, oh no, it’s as light as a feather, and just as delightful.

    Ingridients

    • White baking chocolate
    • Heavy whipping cream: Heavy whipping cream aerates the mousse and gives it that distinct creaminess.
    • Sugar
    • Vanilla extract
    • Mixed fresh berries (optional): While optional, the addition of mixed fresh berries creates a lovely and light contrast to the richness of this White Chocolate Mousse.

    Directions

    1. Step 1: Prepare the mousse base.

      In a small heavy saucepan, combine chocolate and 2/3 cup cream. Cook and stir the mixture over medium-low heat until smooth. Transfer it to a large bowl and cool to room temperature.

    2. Step 2: Whip the heavy cream.

      In a small bowl, beat the remaining cream until it begins to thicken. Add sieved and powdered sugar and vanilla. Beat the mixture until soft peaks form.

    3. Step 3: Fold heavy cream into chocolate mixture.

      Fold 1/4 cup whipped cream into the chocolate mixture, then fold in the remaining whipped cream.

    4. Step 4: Spoon into serving dish(es) and chill.

      Refrigerate while covered, for at least two hours. If desired, garnish with berries.
